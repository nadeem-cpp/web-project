Toba Tek Singh
By Saadat Hasan Manto

Kanika Sachdeva:
10 months ago

Toba tek Singh, a hilarious satirical short story doesn't fail to engage it's readers with its humorous spell. A quick read, almost in one go , the story is what about you find yourself after reading. Impressive urdu words with a tint of hinglish renders the readers engaged and keeps boredom at bay. The use of catchphrase, a whimsical Hindi-arabic cum Punjabi phrase is the most pivotal attraction of the story. Not a must read, but a must try! :).
